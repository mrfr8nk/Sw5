Admission Requirements

Below are the requirements for our O-Level and A-Level programs

O-Level (Form 1-4) Requirements

  • Completed primary school education
  • Original and copy of birth certificate
  • Copy of Grade 7 report card or results slip
  • Four (4) recent passport-sized photos
  • Completed application form
  • Medical report from recognized health practitioner
  • Recommendation letter from previous school (if applicable)

Application Deadline: December 1st for the following academic year

A-Level (Form 5-6) Requirements

  • Minimum of 5 O-Level passes including English and Mathematics
  • Original and copy of birth certificate
  • Copy of O-Level certificate or results slip
  • Four (4) recent passport-sized photos
  • Completed application form
  • Medical report from recognized health practitioner
  • Recommendation letter from O-Level school
  • Personal statement (minimum 300 words)

Application Deadline: November 30th for the following academic year
